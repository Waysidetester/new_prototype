Welcome to Prologics

Our long-standing customer base includes hospitals, physicians and ambulatory surgical centers— we support the provision of radiation oncology services, while ensuring that each patient is our first priority.

Prologics is a unique service company that provides specialty radiation oncology services in the U.S. and international markets.

The company was founded in 2005, providing cutting edge clinical and business services to radiation oncology providers.

What distinguishes Prologics is our comprehensive services, management team and expert clinicians, which are unparalleled in the industry.

The company’s mission is to provide industry-leading radiation oncology services that result in positive clinical outcomes and combine these with top notch business services that support hospitals, physicians and ambulatory surgery centers. The provision of high quality radiation oncology services requires a blend of the latest technologies and patient-centered care models that fit today’s consumers. For example, a successful radiation oncology facility needs to be flexible with their hours of operation, provide an aesthetically-pleasing facility, offer the latest technologies and provide consistent “white glove” customer service for people who are seeking cancer treatment.
